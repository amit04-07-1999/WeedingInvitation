# Premium Animated Indian Wedding Invitation Website

## Design Guidelines

### Design References
- Royal Indian wedding invitation cards
- Premium cinematic wedding websites
- Traditional Indian motifs with modern web aesthetics

### Color Palette
- Primary Background: #0B3D2E (Deep Forest Green)
- Secondary Background: #072A1F (Darker Green)
- Gold Accent: #D4A853 (Rich Gold)
- Gold Light: #F0D78C (Light Gold)
- Gold Glow: #FFD700 (Pure Gold for glows)
- Text Primary: #F0D78C (Gold text)
- Text Secondary: #E8D5B7 (Warm cream)
- White: #FFFDF5 (Warm white)

### Typography
- Heading/Names: Playfair Display (serif, elegant)
- Script/Decorative: Great Vibes (handwritten script)
- Body: Cormorant Garamond (elegant serif)
- Accent: Cinzel (royal display)

### Key Component Styles
- Sections: Full viewport, centered content, fade-in on scroll
- Buttons: Gold border, transparent bg, gold text, glow on hover
- Cards: Semi-transparent dark green with gold borders
- Dividers: Gold ornamental images
